What changed & why

Mapped to the proposal recommendations.

  • 1Compact header. Two slim tiers reclaim vertical space; one clear primary CTA replaces competing buttons.
  • 2Concise courses menu. Six categories with one-line descriptors instead of every qualification at once.
  • 3Focused hero. Single message, dual CTA, and visible RTO/CRICOS trust badges.
  • 4Decision-rich course cards. Level, duration, CRICOS & next intake — plus a per-card action.
  • 5Attributed stats. Same numbers, now with a source line for credibility.
  • 6Video facade. Testimonials load only on click — the biggest page-speed win.
  • 7Tidy news + hidden empties. Empty modules (e.g. events) are auto-hidden in the live design.
  • 8Right-sized chat & clean footer. Smaller launcher, guiding placeholder, high-contrast close; no decorative footer background.
